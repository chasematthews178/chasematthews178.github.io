Jasper AI
Editor's PickMarketing Copy
★★★★★ 4.8
from $39/mo
+

Jasper is the tool I was most skeptical about and the one I ended up using the most. It's trained specifically on marketing content — not general text, but the kind of copy that actually converts.

The brand voice feature is genuinely impressive. Feed it examples of your writing and it learns how you sound. My clients couldn't tell the difference between my drafts and Jasper-assisted ones.

My honest takeThe learning curve is real. Spend your first week on prompting. Once fluent, it's the single biggest time-saver here. Don't expect magic on day one — and fact-check everything.

Who should buy it: Businesses publishing more than 4 pieces of content per month.

What works

  • 60+ templates for every format
  • Surfer SEO integration
  • Team features at scale

What doesn't

  • Requires paid subscription

Common Questions

FAQ

Do I actually need all seven? +
No. Pick the one that solves your single biggest bottleneck. Use it for 30 days. Then consider adding a second. The quiz at the top will tell you where to start.
I'm a solo operator. Are these overkill? +
Solo operators often get the most value from AI tools because your time is your only resource. Start with the free tools — Canva, Kit, and Zapier all have genuinely useful free tiers.
What's the best free starting stack? +
Canva (free) + Zapier (free) + Kit (free to 1,000 subscribers). A complete marketing and automation system for zero dollars.
Will people know my content was AI-assisted? +
If you use AI as a first draft and edit with your own voice — no. Treat AI output as a starting point. Add specific examples, remove anything generic, make sure your actual opinions come through.
